Teaching Philosophy

How I teach

Teaching, for me, is not about transferring information - it is about building intuition. I believe every complex concept has a simple core waiting to be uncovered. My approach centers on starting with the "why", building mental models, and then grounding those models in concrete examples. Whether it is calculus, statistics, or machine learning - my goal is always the same: make the student feel that they understand, not just that they memorized.
